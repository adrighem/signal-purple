# Code of conduct

We want a focused, welcoming, and technically honest project. Be respectful,
assume good faith, accept constructive review, and do not harass, discriminate,
threaten, or publish another person's private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove abusive content and temporarily or permanently
exclude participants whose conduct harms the community. Report conduct issues
privately to the maintainer through the contact method in their GitHub profile.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project.
